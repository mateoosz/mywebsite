The purpose of this page

As a participant in Correlation One Software Development Course, I have created a website to showcase my web development skills and experience. The purpose of this website is to provide a platform for potential employers and clients to learn more about me and my capabilities, as well as to highlight some of the projects I have worked on. On this website, visitors will find a well-organized and visually appealing homepage that introduces me and provides some background information about my education and work experience. There is also a contact form and link to my social media profiles, making it easy for visitors to contact me and learn more about me and my professional background. In addition to the homepage, there are is a subpage that provides more detail about my skills and experience in web development. The page contains some of the projects I have worked on. There is also a highlight of some of the specific web development skills I have acquired, such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Overall, the purpose of this website is to demonstrate my expertise in web development and to provide a platform for potential employers and clients to learn more about me and my work. By showcasing my skills and experience in a well-designed and informative website, I hope to attract new opportunities and advance my career in the field of web development.
